Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical development project take?

The duration of a project depends on its scope and complexity. A simple mobile app can take 2-3 months, while a complex game or enterprise application may require 6-12 months or more.

What is your pricing model?

We offer various pricing models including fixed-price projects, time and materials, and dedicated team arrangements. We'll work with you to find the best fit for your budget and project needs.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support packages including maintenance, updates, and ongoing optimization to ensure your application continues to perform well.

Can you help with app store submission?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience with both Apple App Store and Google Play Store submission processes, including preparing all necessary assets, descriptions, and metadata.
